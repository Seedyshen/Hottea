Verification

Your digital passport to real connections

A verified profile on Kismia means you're a real person — more than just a blue tick, it's a mark of authenticity. For your safety and that of other users, we encourage everyone to get verified profile, potential matches can trust they're interacting with an authentic person.

Our verification process is both fast and secure. Simply follow the instructions to take a new selfie, which won't appear on your profile. This photo is used solely to confirm your identity, using advanced technology and support from our moderation team.

Once you've successfully verified, the grey tick beside your name will turn blue, letting potential matches know you're verified. This adds confidence and ease to new connections on Kismia.

Community Guidelines

Your key to a safe and respectful experience

We're dedicated to creating a welcoming and respectful community for all. To ensure everyone's safety, please adhere to these guidelines.

Behavior

• Be polite.

Being courteous creates a positive and welcoming environment.

• Be authentic.

Honest and genuine photos showcase the real you, building trust and meaningful connections.

• Stay vigilant.

The online world can sometimes be misleading. If you encounter someone suspicious, don't hesitate to report them.

• Enjoy the experience.

Our platform is designed to help you discover exciting new opportunities — so make the most of it!

Our app has a zero-tolerance policy for the following:

  • • Deception or fraud.
  • • Bullying, insults, or threats.
  • • Spam or advertising.
  • • Promotion or distribution of drugs.
  • • Sexual harassment.
  • • Encouraging suicide or self-harm.
  • • Incitement of hatred based on race, nationality, gender, or other attributes.
  • • Using the platform for purposes other than connection and communication.

Profile

• Age.

Use your real birthdate. You must be at least 18 years old to create an account. Accounts created by anyone under 18 will be terminated.

• Photos.

Upload clear, high-quality photos that show your face. Avoid using excessive filters, blurs, and do not include text or contact information in your pictures. Photos of children are also not permitted.

• Username.

Use your real name or a shortened version of it. Avoid offensive words, celebrity names, or symbols.

Content

Our app has a zero-tolerance policy for the following types of content:

  • • Nudity or sexually explicit material.
  • • Content involving minors.
  • • Violent or shocking content.

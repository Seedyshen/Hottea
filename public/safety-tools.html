Safety Tools

Features that ensure your safety

Blocking and Reporting a Story

  1. Open the Story you want to block or report.
  2. Tap the three dots in the upper-right corner of the screen.
  3. Select "Block" or "Report."
  4. Choose a reason and add optional comments if needed.

Additional Safety Measures

  • HotTea uses manual moderation to detect policy violations.
  • Our support team is available 24/7 and ready to assist you.
  • You can report any suspected violations of our Community Guidelines.
  • We respond quickly to violations and may terminate accounts if necessary.
  • We may also take into consideration user behavior outside the platform when deciding to terminate accounts.

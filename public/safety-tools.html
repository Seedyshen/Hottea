Safety Tools

Features that ensure your safety

Blocking and reporting a user

  1. Open the profile of the user you want to block and report, or navigate to your chat with them.
  2. Tap on the three dots in the upper-right corner of the screen.
  3. Select "Block" or "Report."
  4. Choose a reason and add optional comments if needed.

Reporting messages

  1. Open the chat with the user.
  2. Find the message you want to report and long-press it.
  3. Tap "Report."
  4. Choose a reason and add optional comments if needed.

Note: Messages flagged as inappropriate by our AI will be automatically hidden. You can view them by tapping the hidden message.

Verifying your profile

  1. Open your profile page.
  2. Tap the "Verification" icon next to your name.
  3. Follow the verification steps (match the pose shown in the example image and take a selfie with your face clearly visible).

OR:

  1. Open your profile page.
  2. Tap the pencil icon in the upper-right corner of the screen.
  3. Tap the "Get verified" button.
  4. Follow the verification steps (match the pose shown in the example image and take a selfie with your face clearly visible).

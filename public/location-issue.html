Troubleshooting location accuracy problems

If you discover that the city shown in your profile is incorrect, it's likely because the HotTea app doesn't have access to your location. In such cases, your location is determined using IP address settings, which can be somewhat inaccurate.

To check your location access permissions, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to the "Settings" section on your iPhone.

2. Find and select the "Privacy & Security" subsection.

3. Tap on "Location Services."

4. In the list of your apps, find HotTea and ensure that location access for the HotTea app is set to "Always."

Additionally, make sure to enable the "Precise Location" option ‚Äî this ensures that your profile displays the most accurate location, enhancing your ability to connect with people nearby.

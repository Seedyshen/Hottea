Your digital passport to real connections

A verified profile on HotTea means you're a real person.

It’s more than just a blue tick — it's a mark of authenticity. For your safety and that of other users, every female user will be placed on the waitlist to complete photo verification.

Our verification process is fast and secure. Simply follow the instructions to take a new selfie, which won’t appear on your profile. This photo is used solely for identity confirmation, and our moderation team will verify it within 24 hours.

Once you’ve successfully verified, the grey tick beside your name will turn blue, allowing you to interact on HotTea with more confidence, ease, and authenticity.
